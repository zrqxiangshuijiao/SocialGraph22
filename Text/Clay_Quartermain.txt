Quartermain later became part of the [[U.S. military]]'s \"Hulkbusters\" operation, which attempted to capture and contain the [[Hulk]].\n\nHe became a fugitive and traveled with [[Rick Jones (comics)|Rick Jones]] and the Hulk. He met his brother, a farmer. He testified in Congress about a mass murder committed by the [[supervillain]] known as the [[Leader (comics)|Leader]] who had detonated a gamma bomb in a town to obliterate.<ref>''The Incredible Hulk'' (vol. 2) #337-343 (November 1987-April 1988)</ref>\n\nOnce more at S.H.I.E.L.D., Quartermain, along with many other agents, was seemingly killed by a self-aware, renegade \"Deltan\" variety of the agency's [[Android (robot)|artificial-human]] \"[[Life Model Decoy]]s\", and replaced by one such LMD.<ref>''Nick Fury vs. S.H.I.E.L.D.'' #2 (July 1988)</ref> The most advanced of the Deltite LMDs, it rebelled upon learning it was not human, and after several confrontations, perished.<ref>''Nick Fury vs. S.H.I.E.L.D.'' #6 (November 1988)</ref>\n\n[[Retconned]] as a former romantic interest of [[Jessica Jones]], Quartermain later became leader of [[Nick Fury's Howling Commandos|S.H.I.E.L.D.'s Paranormal Containment Unit]]. He was the agent sent to inform Jennifer Walters ([[She-Hulk]]) that she had been pressed into service as a S.H.I.E.L.D. agent.<ref>''She-Hulk'' (vol. 2) #14 (January 2007)</ref> He next led a new incarnation of S.H.I.E.L.D.'s \"Hulkbusters\" unit, here She-Hulk, Agent Crimson, and [[Agent Cheesecake]].<ref>''She-Hulk'' (vol. 2) #15 (March 2007)</ref> Clay and his team then defeated the electrical entity [[Zzzax]].<ref>''She-Hulk'' (vol. 2) #17 (April 2007)</ref>\n\nQuartermain remained an ally of Jones, using her to bring down a conspiracy against the [[President of the United States]].<ref>''Alias'' #1-4 (November 2001-February 2002)</ref> He assisted Jessica in the investigation of [[Spider-Woman (Mattie Franklin)|Mattie Franklin]], a young female superhero trapped in mysterious circumstances.<ref>''Alias'' #16 (January 2003)</ref> Quartermain later led a S.H.I.E.L.D. unit that rescued Jones from a HYDRA recruitment attempt.<ref>''[[The Pulse (comics)|The Pulse]]'' #6-9 (2005)</ref> A flashback reveals Clay befriended Jessica while she was recovering from a months-long ordeal with the Purple Man.<ref>''Alias'' #26 (November 2003)</ref>\n\nAt a [[meatpacking]] plant, Quartermain was found, apparently dead in a suspected Red Hulk attack. The commanding officer of the crime scene was his old friend [[Gabe Jones]].<ref>''Hulk'' (vol. 2) #4 (June 2008)</ref> It was later revealed, however, that his actual murderer was the now-insane [[Doc Samson]]. Quartermain had inadvertently stumbled upon a plot hatched by Red Hulk and Samson, and threatened to launch a full-scale investigation.
