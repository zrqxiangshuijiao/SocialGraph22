The character is usually depicted as a member of the [[Serpent Society]]. He first appeared in ''[[Captain America (comic book)|Captain America]]'' vol. 1 #341 in May 1988, created by writer [[Mark Gruenwald]] and artist [[Kieron Dwyer]]. He has super hard bones, skin and muscles, and uses specially designed hand grenades that entangle his opponents in steel ribbons.\n\nHe was first introduced as a henchman of [[Viper (Madame Hydra)|Viper]] as she took control of the [[Serpent Society]], deposing [[Sidewinder (comics)|Sidewinder]] as the leader of the Society. Viper's ploy to take control of the United States was foiled by [[Captain America]] and members of the Society still loyal to Sidewinder. Rock Python was allowed to remain with the Society after Viper was defeated.
